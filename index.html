Common Questions

FAQ

How far in advance do I need to book?

+

We can often accommodate same-day bookings, especially for sessions during regular hours. For specific therapist requests or weekend evenings, we recommend booking 24–48 hours in advance.

What areas do you cover?

+

We cover all Nairobi neighborhoods including Westlands, Karen, Kilimani, Kileleshwa, Lavington, Parklands, Runda, Muthaiga, Upperhill, CBD, and surrounding areas. Message us if you're unsure.

Is it safe to invite a therapist to my home?

+

Absolutely. Every therapist undergoes a thorough background check, professional certification verification, and in-person interview before joining our team. We take your safety and privacy very seriously.

When do I pay?

+

Payment is collected only after your session is complete. We accept cash, M-Pesa, and card payments. There's no upfront deposit required for standard bookings.

What do I need to prepare for the therapist?

+

Simply clear enough space for a massage table (or the floor for Thai massage), have a towel or two available, and let us know in advance if there are any special requirements. Our therapists bring all professional equipment.

Can I request a specific therapist?

+

Yes! Browse our therapist profiles and mention your preference when booking. We'll do our best to match you with your requested therapist subject to availability.
